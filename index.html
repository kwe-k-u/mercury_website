Mercury is a part of the Duala ecosystem and a guiding principle of Duala is utmost respect for the privacy of our users. As such, we do not sell any of your data to anybody else. The only data we collect by default is data that allows us to properly provide the essential features of our projects. Unless you voluntarily give us your data for any purposes beyond this, we do not collect it.

If you delete your Mercury account, all data associated with your account is deleted from our platform.
